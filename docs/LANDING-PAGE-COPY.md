## 1. Hero Headline

## 2. Subheadline

One simple dashboard to manage your temporary workers, schedules, and hours. Built for staffing coordinators who are tired of juggling Excel files and emails.

---

## 3. Problem Section

**Sound familiar?**

- **Scattered data everywhere** — Worker info split across multiple spreadsheets, emails, and chat messages. No single source of truth.
- **Hours wasted on scheduling** — Finding available workers for open shifts means digging through files and making calls. Every assignment risks errors.
- **Reporting is a nightmare** — Generating worked hours for payroll takes hours of manual work, with no audit trail when questions arise.

---

## 4. Solution Section

**One place for everything:**

- **See all workers at a glance** — Know who's available, who's assigned, and where everyone is working. No more hunting through spreadsheets.
- **Fill shifts in minutes** — Filter by availability, assign workers, and track every change. All in one place.
- **Generate reports instantly** — Export worked hours to CSV or Excel for payroll and billing. Complete audit trail included.

---

## 5. How It Works

1. **Add your workers and clients** — Import your existing data or add them manually. Takes minutes, not hours.
2. **Create and manage schedules** — Assign workers to shifts with our visual scheduling board. Filter, sort, and find the right person fast.
3. **Track hours and export** — All worked hours logged automatically. Export reports whenever you need them.
